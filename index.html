  • Congratulations, you've been put forward for the nology sheep herding contest! Using the Figma designs as a guide, utilise your Flexbox skills to arrange the sheep in the correct order.

  • Feel free to look, but do not modify the index.html or base-style.css files
  • Make sure to pay close attention to the order and space the sheep need for each level!
  • Each level contains exactly one flex container
  • The further you go, the harder the challenges get, and the more flex properties you'll have to use!
